How To Play

Move

WASD or Arrows

Jump

SPACE or Z

Dash

X or V

💨 Orbs can be collected while dashing.

💡 Collect 3 Orbs to reveal the Exit Portal in the starting room.

⚠️ Warning: Collecting all orbs will awaken the BOSS. Escape quickly!
